Banana Sandwich

Making a banana, blackberry, and honey sandwich is easy. All you need is 100gm of sugar, blackberries, honey. 50ml of water, peanuts, 1 table spoon of butter, and two slices of toasted bread.

Pasta with Veggies

Pasta with veggies is a splendid substitute of Bolognese Pasta, Pasta Alfredo, or others. Use your favorite veges like - Spinach, tomato, broccoli, spring onions, etc. for a delightful treat.

Steamed Peas and Olives

As simple as it can get, yet not that simple. The steamed peas, olives, yogurt, and bread combination is one of the simplest yet healthy meal you can have at the beginning of a day. It's low cal but definitely fulfilling.
